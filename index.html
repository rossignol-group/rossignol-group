The Regulatory Context

One of the most urgent things on your plate right now: mandatory climate reporting.

Under the Treasury Laws Amendment Act 2024, Australian entities are now required to report in line with AASB S2 — the local adoption of the ISSB IFRS S2 standard. Reporting phases in across three groups.

Group 1 — Now

Large Entities

First report: 30 June 2026

500+ employees, $1B+ revenue, or $500M+ assets. First AASB S2 reports due for FY2024/25. Scope 1 & 2 assurance required from day one.

Already in motion
Group 2 — Coming Fast

Mid-Market Companies

Reporting from 1 July 2026

250+ employees, $200M+ revenue, or $500M+ assets. Preparation must begin now. This is where Canopy is purpose-built to help.

Group 3 — On the Horizon

Smaller Entities

Reporting from 1 July 2027

Entities not captured by Groups 1 or 2. The window to prepare is shorter than it appears — start understanding your obligations now.

External assurance applies from your first reporting period. Canopy is purpose-built to get you there.
