Our Model

Platform-first. Expert-supported.

Rossignol's advisory services are designed to complement Canopy, not replace it. The platform does the structural work — gathering, mapping, and drafting. Our experts step in where the standard gets nuanced, where your situation is unusual, or where an auditor will ask harder questions.

We work on a scoped engagement basis. No retainers, no lock-in. You engage us for what you need, when you need it — and we bring in the right specialist for each task.

What We Offer

Expert services for every stage of your climate journey.

Each engagement is scoped to your specific situation. We coordinate the right expertise — no unnecessary overhead.

AASB S2 Readiness Assessment

A structured gap analysis of where you stand against AASB S2 requirements — what data you have, what's missing, and a clear roadmap before reporting begins.

Scope 3 Emissions Mapping

Scope 3 is the hardest part of any climate disclosure. We map your value chain, identify your most material emission categories, and build a defensible methodology for quantification.

Climate Scenario Analysis

We facilitate structured scenario analysis across recognised climate pathways and frame the outputs clearly for your AASB S2 disclosure narrative.

Assurance Preparation

External assurance is mandatory from your first report. We prepare your disclosure package for ASSA 5000 limited assurance — working alongside your auditor to make sure everything holds up.

Sustainability Strategy

For organisations ready to go further — we develop sustainability strategies aligned with your business objectives and broader reporting frameworks including GRI, SASB, and CDP.

How We Engage

A process designed around your situation.

Every engagement starts with understanding your specific obligations, your existing data, and your timeline — not a generic checklist.

1

Discovery Call

We spend 30–45 minutes understanding your company, your reporting tranche, your existing data, and your timeline. No obligation. We'll tell you honestly what you need.

2

Scoped Proposal

Based on the discovery call, we provide a clear, fixed-scope proposal. You know exactly what you're getting, what it costs, and when you'll have it.

3

Engagement & Delivery

We engage the right specialists for your scope — whether that's climate risk modellers, emissions accountants, or assurance advisors — and manage the process end to end.

4

Handover to Canopy

Where advisory work feeds into your disclosure, outputs are structured to integrate directly with Canopy — so nothing gets lost between the expert engagement and your final report.
