Our Mission

Rossignol was founded on the belief that most organisations don't lack ambition — they lack a clear, executable plan. We exist to provide the strategic rigour to close that gap, at a moment when the cost of getting AI strategy wrong has never been higher.

Who We Are

An AI strategy advisory firm. Small by design. Senior by default.

Rossignol is a principal-led advisory firm focused on AI strategy and organisational design. We work with ambitious organisations — scale-ups, established businesses, and boards — to assess where they are, design where they need to go, and provide the strategic advice to get there.

Our approach is grounded in rigorous methodology drawn from leading academic and industry research on AI strategy, operating model design, and responsible governance. We apply it with discipline — not as a template, but as a thinking framework that produces better outcomes in each specific context we work in.

We're deliberately small. Every engagement is led by a principal. There are no junior teams doing the work while a partner takes the credit. What you see is what you get.

What Drives Us

Three things we believe deeply.

Clarity First

Strategy before technology, always.

The worst AI implementations start with a tool looking for a problem. We start with the problem — and work backwards to the right solution, even if that solution isn't AI.

Execution

A plan that doesn't get implemented is just a document.

We design for execution from the start. That means being honest about constraints, building in change management, and staying engaged until the work is actually done.

Independence

Good advice requires no conflict of interest.

We have no financial relationship with any AI vendor, platform, or technology provider. Our recommendations are driven entirely by what's right for the client. That independence is foundational — not a selling point.
