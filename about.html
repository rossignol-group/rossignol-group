Who We Are

A sustainability consultancy built for what Australian business actually needs right now.

Rossignol was built to sit at the intersection of sustainability expertise and practical technology. We saw the gap: a significant regulatory obligation landing on thousands of Australian mid-market companies, with no accessible, affordable way to meet it.

Our response is Canopy — an intelligent platform that turns mandatory climate reporting from a burden into a structured, manageable process. Wrapped around it is a team of advisors who know when the platform needs a human hand.

We believe sustainability reporting done well isn't just about compliance. It forces organisations to understand their climate exposures, think rigorously about their strategy, and build the internal capability to act.

What Drives Us

Three things we believe deeply.

Accessibility

Compliance shouldn't require a Big 4 budget.

Mid-market companies face the same legal obligations as ASX 20 giants, without the same resources. Canopy exists to level that playing field.

Rigour

Good disclosure is good business.

A well-constructed AASB S2 report reveals real climate exposures, sharpens strategy, and builds investor confidence. We help companies get there with substance, not just form.

Action

Reporting is a starting point, not the destination.

A disclosure is a moment of clarity. We help organisations understand what their climate data is telling them — and use it to make better decisions.
